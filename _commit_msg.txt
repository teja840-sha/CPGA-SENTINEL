Fix double-numbered section headings + missing Figure 1

Regenerate paper/CPGA_SENTINEL_preprint.pdf after fixing two bugs in
the pandoc->LaTeX postprocessor:

* Section headings were rendered as "1 1. Introduction" because the
  manually-numbered headings from the HTML source were kept inside
  \section{} arguments while LaTeX auto-numbered them as well. The
  postprocessor now strips leading dotted-number prefixes ("1. ",
  "6.9.1.2 ", etc.) from section/subsection/subsubsection arguments.
* The Figure 1 architecture diagram placeholder never got replaced
  because pandoc had escaped the underscores to "FIGURE\_1\_PLACEHOLDER"
  and the regex was matching the unescaped form. Regex now accepts
  either form, so the architecture figure environment is now emitted.

No content changes. Recompiled PDF is 29 pp, 544 KB, fonts embedded,
all 39 \citep{refN} resolve, no undefined references.
